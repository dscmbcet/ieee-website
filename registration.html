Registration Details

Once your paper is accepted the registration phase begins. The last date for registration is 25th January 2022 Please follow the following steps for completing the registration process.

Step 1 - Registration Fee Payment

It is mandatory that at least one of the authors should register in order to include the paper in the technical programme. If an author has got more than one accepted papers, each paper has to be registered separately.

Maximum page limit of a paper is 6 pages in 2 column IEEE Conference paper style. The papers exceeding 6 pages (a maximum of 8 pages) should pay extra page charge of Rs.1000/- (Indian authors) or 20 USD (Foreign authors)
